Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account with Wellness Coach, we collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, age, gender, fitness goals
  • Health Data: Height, weight, medical conditions (optional)
  • Contact Information: Email address for communication

Usage Information

We automatically collect information about how you interact with our service:

  • Device information (type, operating system)
  • Log data (IP address, browser type)
  • Usage patterns (features used, time spent)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

How We Use Your Data

Service Provision

We use your information to create and maintain your account, provide personalized wellness plans, and deliver the services you request.

Improvement & Analytics

We analyze usage patterns to improve our services, develop new features, and enhance user experience.

Communication

We may send you service-related announcements, updates, and support messages. You can opt-out of marketing emails at any time.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data

Wellness Coach is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Important: We only share your information in the following limited circumstances:

Legal Requirements

When required by law or to respond to legal processes

Service Providers

Trusted third parties who assist in providing our services (with strict data protection agreements)

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

Encryption

All data transmitted is encrypted using SSL/TLS

Secure Storage

Data stored in secure, access-controlled databases

Access Control

Strict access controls and authentication

Your Role in Security

Use a strong password, enable two-factor authentication when available, and never share your login credentials.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

Access & Portability

Request a copy of your personal data

Correction

Update or correct inaccurate information

Deletion

Request deletion of your personal data

Opt-Out

Opt-out of marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@wellnesscoach.com.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and personalize content.

You can control cookie settings through your browser. Note that disabling cookies may affect some features of our service.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes.

Questions About Our Privacy Policy?

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact our Privacy Team.
