Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Wellness Coach. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our support team.

Creating an account is simple! Click the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation or visit our registration page. You'll need to provide:

  • Your full name
  • Email address
  • Password (minimum 8 characters with letters and numbers)

After registration, you'll receive a confirmation email. Click the link to verify your account.

If you haven't received the password reset email, try these steps:

  1. Check your spam/junk folder - Sometimes emails get filtered incorrectly
  2. Wait 5 minutes - Email delivery can sometimes be delayed
  3. Verify your email address - Make sure you entered the correct email associated with your account
  4. Resend the reset link - Use the "Resend Email" button on the reset page

If you still don't receive the email after 15 minutes, please contact our support team.

For security reasons, password reset links are valid for 1 hour from the time they are sent. If the link expires, you can request a new one by clicking "Resend Email" on the reset page.

Security Tip: Always use the latest reset link. Old links become invalid once a new one is generated.

Yes, you have full control over your data. You can delete your account at any time:

  1. Go to your Account Settings
  2. Scroll to the bottom and click "Delete Account"
  3. Confirm your choice by entering your password
  4. All your personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, progress, and history will be lost.

Yes, you can change your email address by following these steps:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to Account Settings
  3. Click "Edit" next to your email address
  4. Enter your new email address and current password
  5. Check your new email for a verification link
  6. Click the link to confirm the change

Note: After changing your email, you'll need to use the new email address to log in.
